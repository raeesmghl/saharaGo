FAQ's

What is included in the desert safari tour?

The tour includes dune bashing, camel riding, cultural entertainment (belly dancing, Tanoura dance, fire show), a BBQ dinner, henna painting, sandboarding, a visit to a Bedouin camp, and stargazing. Transfers from and to your hotel are also included.

How long does the desert safari tour last?

The tour typically lasts around 6 hours, starting in the late afternoon and ending in the evening. This allows for ample time to experience all the activities and enjoy the desert sunset.

What should I wear for the desert safari?

We recommend wearing comfortable, lightweight clothing and closed-toe shoes. A hat, sunglasses, and sunscreen are also advisable. During the winter months, bring a light jacket as it can get chilly in the evening.

What if I have dietary restrictions or allergies?

Please let us know in advance if you have any dietary restrictions or allergies. We will do our best to accommodate your needs and ensure you have a safe and enjoyable dining experience.
